About Phraisins

Phraisins is a free word game where players have four tries to guess a hidden phrase with the help of a clue. You can play up to three puzzles a day. Correct guesses win you raisins, unlock riddles, and raise you to new levels of grapeness. The game is designed by Eli Stutz for your pure, fruity enjoyment.

Also try Wizarding Words and Star Words, themed twists on the daily puzzle.
